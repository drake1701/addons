LevelFlight - Lets you fly level.
	by Cidrei (Jazrei of Zangarmarsh-US)
---

Usage:
-- Bind LevelFlight to a key in the key bindings. Pressing the key will force you to fly level. Pressing it again will allow you to fly free once more.
-- You can also create a macro containing "/click LevelFlightButton" to toggle level flight.
-- There is a simple configuration section at the top of LevelFlight.lua.
